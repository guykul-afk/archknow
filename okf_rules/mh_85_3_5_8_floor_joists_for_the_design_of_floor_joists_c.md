## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 85

5.8 Floor joists 
For the design of floor joists, calculations should be prepared 
for final design by a qualified engineer in accordance with BS 
EN 1995–1–1: 2004 + A2: 2014 Eurocode 5 and PD 6693–1: 
2019.
