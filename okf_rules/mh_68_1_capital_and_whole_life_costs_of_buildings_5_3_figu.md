# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 68

Capital and whole life costs of buildings 5-3 
Figure 5.2 looks at financial risk and who owns it for the most 
common procurement options. Management of risk is an ongoing 
process throughout the life of the project, as risks will be con-
stantly changing. Risk management plans should be in place to 
deal quickly and effectively with risks if they arise. It is important 
to work as an integrated project team from the earliest possible 
stages on an open book basis to identify risks throughout the 
team’s supply chains.
