# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 72

• Capital asset replacement. These whole life costs studies focus 
solely on modelling the operational life of durable assets such as 
mechanical systems; roof finishes etc., that require replacement 
during the life of the building. These enable the client to plan for 
long-term maintenance obligations.
