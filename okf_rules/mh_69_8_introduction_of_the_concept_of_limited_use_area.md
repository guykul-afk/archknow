# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 69

• Introduction of the concept of limited use area. This includes 
areas with limited height (less than 1.5 metres), areas with 
limited natural light, internal structural walls/columns, area 
difference between internal dominant face and the wall/floor 
junction. Whilst these areas are included in the measurement, 
they must also be stated separately. 
It will take the wider market some time to embrace IPMS, espe-
cially with the impact on end user market for valuation purposes, 
as it will have a direct impact on their comparable databases. There 
will be a period where the existing GEA, GIA and NIA will remain 
in favour.
