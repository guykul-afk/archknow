# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 61

4.1 Scope 
Many elements, such as the design of religious facilities, trans-
port infrastructure, master planning, schools, libraries as well as 
features such as stair design and so forth are detailed in the other 
chapters of the Metric Handbook. The following advice supple-
ments the other chapters and follows the macro to the minutia of 
the design process.
