# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 86

6.2 Specification 
Provided a clear specification is prepared and concrete placement 
is checked by site staff, the concrete should fulfil its function 
indefinitely. This specification should be in accordance with BS 
EN 8500–1: 2015 + A2: 2019 Complementary British Standard to 
BS EN 206. Method of specifying and guidance for the specifier 
and BS EN 8500–2: 2015: + A2: 2019. Complementary British 
Standard to BS EN 206. Specification for constituent materials 
and concrete, which cover not only strength requirements but also 
minimum cement content, aggregate size, cement type and other 
relevant aspects. BS EN 206: 2013 + A1: 2016 gives guidance on 
specification, production, performance and conformity.
