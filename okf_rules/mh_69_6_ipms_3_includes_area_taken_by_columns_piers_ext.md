# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 69

• IPMS 3 includes area taken by columns, piers/external col-
umns and window reveals. All of these are excluded under NIA. 
Additionally, space taken by balconies, covered galleries, roof 
terraces are all included but stated separately. Lift lobbies and 
area with headroom of less than 1.5 metres are also included but 
stated separately as limited use area.
