# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 86

• To ensure sufficient protection in case of fire. 
Tables A.10, A.12 and A.13 of BS 8500–1: 2015 + A2: 2019 
give limiting values for the nominal cover of concrete made with 
normal weight aggregates. In no case should the cover be less than 
the nominal maximum aggregate size, or, for the main reinforce-
ment, the bar size.
