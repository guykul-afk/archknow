# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 64

5.2 Public toilets 
The provision of public toilets allows many people to spend more 
time in an area, to dwell and linger and enjoy the space. Toilets 
are not just important for families with small children, for preg-
nant women, some disabled people and for older people, but for the 
seven million people in the UK who have an over-active bladder.
