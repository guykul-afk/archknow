# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 64

5.4 Faith 
Where WCs are provided, they should not be directly adjacent to 
prayer rooms. 
A proportion of WC pans and urinals should not align with 
Mecca. Quibla, the direction of the Kaaba (the sacred building at 
Mecca) to which Muslims turn and pray, should be established at 
the earliest RIBA work stage possible to assist with the setting out 
of sanitary facilities.
