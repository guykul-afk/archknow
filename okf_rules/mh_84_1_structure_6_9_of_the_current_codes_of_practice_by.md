# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 84

Structure 6-9 
of the current Codes of Practice by non-specialists is not recom-
mended. In complicated timber structures, the sizes of the members 
tend to depend more on the design of the connections than on the 
internal stresses. 
Design of timber should be carried out in accordance with 
the Eurocode for timber, BS EN 1995–1–1: 2004 + A2: 2014, 
Eurocode 5. Design of timber structures. General. Common rules 
and rules for buildings.
