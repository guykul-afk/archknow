# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 72

• An estimate of the operational cost of an asset. In this instance, 
the whole life cost study should cover all potential sources of cost 
associated with the occupation of a building. These cost centres 
might include energy, cleaning, insurances, maintenance and so 
on. Total operating cost assessments such as these are typically 
used by clients to confirm that they will be able to afford to run 
and maintain their assets.
