# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 89

8.2 Design 
The structural use of glass requires an understanding of the behav-
iour of both the structure and the material. The inherent brittle 
nature of glass dictates consideration of the nature and conse-
quences of any failure modes. 
The choice of international standards for the design of edge sup-
ported glass panels is wide-ranging, but BS 6262–1: 2017, Glazing 
for buildings. General methodology for the selection of glazing, is 
the current British Standard.
