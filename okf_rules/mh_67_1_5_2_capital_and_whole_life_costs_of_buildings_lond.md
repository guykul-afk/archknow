# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 67

5-2 Capital and whole life costs of buildings 
London, may reflect in an increase in the exchange value of the 
neighbouring buildings. 
Whilst not all sources of value have an immediate financial dimen-
sion, all require a focused and disciplined approach by the design 
team to concentrate effort and investment on aspects of the design 
which maximise benefits to client, end users and the wider commu-
nity, without compromising the fundamental financial viability of 
the project.
