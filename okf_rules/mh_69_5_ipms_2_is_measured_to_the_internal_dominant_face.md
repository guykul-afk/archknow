# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 69

• IPMS 2 is measured to the internal dominant face. The inter-
nal dominant face is defined as the ‘surface comprising more 
than 50% of the surface area for each vertical section forming an 
internal perimeter’. So, for example, for an office building with 
unitised cladding, this would mean the glass surface. Balconies 
and covered canopies would be stated separately.
