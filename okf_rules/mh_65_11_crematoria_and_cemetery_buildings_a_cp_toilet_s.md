# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 65

• Crematoria and cemetery buildings. 
A CP toilet should be at least 3 m wide and 4 m long, and have 
a ceiling height of at least 2.4 m to accommodate the hoist. The 
entrance door should provide an effective clear width of 1000 mm.
