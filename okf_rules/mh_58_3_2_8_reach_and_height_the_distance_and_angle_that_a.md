## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 58

2.8 Reach and height 
The distance and angle that an individual can reach is dependent on 
their size, agility, dexterity and whether they are seated or stand-
ing. The ability of a seated person, for example, in a wheelchair to 
reach forward, sideways, upwards or downwards is different from 
someone standing.
