# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 64

• Staff may need to be suitably trained in evacuation features and 
procedures including ‘carry up’ and ‘carry down’ procedures for 
evacuation of wheelchair users and other disabled people.
