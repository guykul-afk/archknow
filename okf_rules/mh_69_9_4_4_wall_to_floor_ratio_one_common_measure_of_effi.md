4.4 Wall to floor ratio 
One common measure of efficiency is the wall to floor ratio for 
the building. The wall to floor ratio of a building is calculated by 
dividing the external wall area by the gross internal floor area. This 
indicates the proportion of external wall required to enclose a given 
floor area Figure 5.4 shows the ratio can affect the cost of a tall 
office tower.
