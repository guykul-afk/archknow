# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 84

5.4 Timber sources and grades 
Timber can be home-produced or imported. There is a degree of 
standardisation, but the designer can encounter a wide variation 
in qualities and where appropriate it should have been classified 
to the necessary strength class. Timber should always be accessed 
from certified environmentally managed and sustainable sources.
