# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 70

• Cost information published by third parties, including price 
books, journal articles and so on. 
In order to normalise this diverse range of information for loca-
tion, price inflation and so forth, cost consultants apply a range 
of adjustment factors derived from the statistical analysis of large 
project data-sets, undertaken by bodies such as the Building Cost 
Information Service (BCIS) or large practices who have their own 
in-house research teams.
