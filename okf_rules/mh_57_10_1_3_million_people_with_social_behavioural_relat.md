# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 57

• 1.3 million people with social/behavioural related disability. 
The RNID has identified that in the UK there are 12 million 
adults with hearing loss greater than 25 dBHL. This is equivalent 
to one in five adults. 
The National Autistic Society indicates that one in 100 people 
are on the autism spectrum. There are around 700000 autistic adults 
and children in the UK.
