# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 77

2.7 Structural elements 
For convenience of design, large structures are broken up into 
elements. These are of different types according to the function 
they perform in the building. Before describing each type, it will 
be necessary to explore the forces that are found internally in the 
materials of the structure.
