# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 62

4.8 m long). Access to the rear of a vehicle by ramp also requires 
extra space. The provision of a larger designated accessible parking 
space 4.8 m wide by 8 m long benefits people using hoists or ramps 
to access their vehicles, and caters for larger commercial vehicles 
converted for side or rear access using hoists or ramps. In cov-
ered parking, allowance should be made for high top conversion 
vehicles and vehicles with roof mounted hoists, with a minimum 
clearance of 2.6 m. 
The number of recommended designated bays should be cal-
culated on the basis of the Table VI, which indicates minimum 
provision and allows for future flexibility if required. The table 
sets out a minimum percentage of bays but also suggests a flexible 
approach to future provision should demand dictate it.
