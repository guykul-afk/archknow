# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 54

• Provide buildings and environments that are convenient and 
enjoyable for everyone to use. 
BS 8300–1:2018 and BS 8300–2:2018 Design of an accessible 
and inclusive built environment – Code of Practice (BS 8300) set 
out the importance of integrating inclusive design principles into 
the development process in order to achieve the desired outcome of 
inclusive and accessible environments. 
Inclusive design is about making places everyone can use. 
The inclusive design process is just as important in the briefing 
and master planning stages as it is in the design of individual 
components/built elements. The built environment should facil-
itate independence, not only in the built form, but also in green 
space strategies, social infrastructure planning, housing and 
transportation.
