# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 58

3.1 General considerations 
It is important to consider the additional space requirements for 
people using mobility aids, people with assistance dogs, and parents 
and carers with children in pushchairs, particularly along circula-
tion routes, barriers/gate lines and spaces required to manoeuvre 
outside lifts or at building elements such as reception desks or 
servery counters.
