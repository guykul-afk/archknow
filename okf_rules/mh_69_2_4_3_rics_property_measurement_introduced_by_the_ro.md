4.3 RICS Property Measurement 
Introduced by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (RICS), 
the RICS Property Measurement replaces the Code of Measuring 
Practice and incorporates the International Property Measurement 
Standards (IPMS) for Offices, Retail Buildings, Industrial Buildings 
and Residential Buildings. The Code of Measuring Practice 6th 
Edition remains in effect for all other building types.
