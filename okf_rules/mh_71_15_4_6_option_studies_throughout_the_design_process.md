# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 71

4.6 Option studies 
Throughout the design process, architects and engineers need to 
examine alternative design solutions, be it structural options, dif-
ferent air conditioning systems or alternative floor finishes. The 
cost consultant can contribute to the selection of preferred solution 
through the preparation of an option study, taking into account the 
full cost implications of each choice, which might include effects 
on the costs of other building elements, overall project duration 
and so on. Increasingly, option studies are prepared on the basis of 
whole life costs, taking into account the operational dimensions of 
specification as well as short-term considerations based on capital 
cost, programme and procurement.
