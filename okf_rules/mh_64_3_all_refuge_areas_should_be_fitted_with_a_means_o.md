# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 64

• All refuge areas should be fitted with a means of two-way 
communication between the person waiting in the refuge and 
a central, monitored location from where evacuation assistance 
will be coordinated (note the communication system should be 
accessible to people who are blind and partially sighted as well 
as people who are deaf, deafened or hard of hearing).
