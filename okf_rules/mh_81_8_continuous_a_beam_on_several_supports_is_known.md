# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 81

• Continuous. A beam on several supports is known as a contin-
uous beam. Generally, the maximum moments occur over the 
supports. Tension in the beam section over the supports occurs 
at the top, but occurs at the bottom at mid-span. Continuous con-
crete beams have to be reinforced accordingly.
