# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 60

4.6 Wheelchair users performing a 90° turn 
Inclusive and accessible design 4-7 
broad groups: those who have some use of both legs and feet, and 
those who have use of only one leg. Those who can use only one 
leg require a handhold wherever there are steps, even at a single 
step at a building threshold.
