# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 77

2.4 Force units 
Forces, including loads, are measured in Newtons (N). One Newton is 
the force required to give a mass of 1 kg an acceleration of 1 m/s2. A 
tip to remember is that a Newton is about the weight of an apple. Most 
forces in structural engineering are expressed in kN (kilo-newtons). 
Table I gives conversions from and to SI, MT and FPS (Imperial) 
units for loadings of all types.
