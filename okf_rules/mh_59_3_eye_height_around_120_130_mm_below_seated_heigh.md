# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 59

• Eye height, around 120–130 mm below seated height, giving a 
5th–95th per cent range for wheelchair users from 960 to 1250 
mm (1080–1315 mm for scooter users)
