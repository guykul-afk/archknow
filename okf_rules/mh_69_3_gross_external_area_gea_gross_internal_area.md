# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 69

• Gross External Area (GEA), Gross Internal Area (GIA) or Net 
Internal Area (NIA) are no longer used. This terminology has 
been replaced with IPMS 1, IPMS 2 and IPMS 3. Whilst these 
are not direct replacements, they can be closely related to GEA, 
GIA and NIA respectively.
