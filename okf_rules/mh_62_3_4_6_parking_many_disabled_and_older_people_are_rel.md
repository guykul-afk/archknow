# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 62

4.6 Parking 
Many disabled and older people are reliant on a car for independ-
ence. Additional space for designated bays for disabled persons is 
vital. The driver of a vehicle or a passenger may be disabled. They 
may need to enter or leave the vehicle using a transfer hoist for 
their wheelchair, or they may require assistance to alight from the 
vehicle first. In some instances, a disabled person may need to drive 
a powered wheelchair or scooter through the rear or side entrance
