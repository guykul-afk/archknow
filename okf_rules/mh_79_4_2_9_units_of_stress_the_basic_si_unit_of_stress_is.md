# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 79

2.9 Units of stress 
The basic SI unit of stress is the N/m2 (which is also called the 
Pascal, Pa), but this is too small a unit for practical purposes. The 
correct form for the normal unit is the mega Pascal, MPa or mega 
Newton MN/m2, but this is expressed by engineers in units of 
N/mm2.
