# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 89

7.3 Grades of steel 
Steel for structural purposes is available in the UK a range of stand-
ard grades increasing in strength: S275, which corresponds to the 
previous description of ‘mild steel’, S355, S460 and S500.
