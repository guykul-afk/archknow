# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 72

• Discount rate – this should take into account the client’s 
requirements. In the case of the public sector, discount rates are 
published by the Treasury, and in the private sector, discount 
rates generally reflect the client’s cost of finance or expecta-
tions for rates of return. As it can have such an impact on the 
end result, the discount rate must always be confirmed by the 
client.
