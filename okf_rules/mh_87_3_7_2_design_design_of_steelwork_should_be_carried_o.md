# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 87

7.2 Design 
Design of steelwork should be carried out in accordance with 
limit state codes based upon plastic theory. Eurocode 3 BS EN 
Table XII Properties of steel and aluminium 
1993–1–1: 2005 + A1: 2014 Design of steel structures. General 
rules and rules for buildings, sets the criteria. 
A book of tables published by the Steel Construction Institute, 
(‘Blue Book’ – Steel building design: Design data – Reprinted 
2015) gives dimensions and section properties of currently man-
ufactured steel sections. Tables XIII, XIV and XV give the 
dimensions for a selection of universal beams, joists and universal 
columns. This book also contains capacity tables which may be 
used to quickly determine the suitability of a particular section for 
preliminary design. More rigorous calculations to BS EN 1993–1– 
1: 2005 + A1: 2014 are generally required for final design.
