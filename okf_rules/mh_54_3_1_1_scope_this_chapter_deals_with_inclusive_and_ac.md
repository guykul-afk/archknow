# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 54

1.1 Scope 
This chapter deals with inclusive and accessible design for 
non-dwellings. For dwellings, refer to Chapter 22 Houses and flats, 
Chapter 23 Housing for older people and Chapter 24 Housing for 
students and young people.
