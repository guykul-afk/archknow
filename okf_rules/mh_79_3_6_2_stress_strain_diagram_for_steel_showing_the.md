# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 79

6.2 Stress – strain diagram for steel showing the yield point 
where Hooke’s Law (stress is proportional to strain) no longer 
operates. Dotted line shows deformation caused when stress 
is reduced after the yield point, material does not return to its 
original form
