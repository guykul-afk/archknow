# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 82

4.2 Design 
Masonry should be designed in accordance with the British 
Standard BS EN Eurocode 6 – Design of masonry structures 
Part 3: Simplified calculation methods for unreinforced masonry 
structures, supported by PD 6697: 2019 Recommendations for the 
design of masonry structures to BS EN 1996–1–1: 2005 + A1: 
2012 and BS EN 1996–2: 2006. 
The Institution of Structural Engineers Manual for the design of 
plain masonry in building structures to Eurocode 6, 2018 (second 
edition) offers design guidance for simple structural masonry.
