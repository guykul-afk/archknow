# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 66

• Brand/organisation value. Messages communicated by a build-
ing derived from its design, and which reflect positively on the 
occupier. For example, a flagship development designed to 
achieve exemplary standards of sustainability and environmen-
tal performance.
