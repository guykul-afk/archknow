# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 86

6.7 Effective depth 
The effective depth of a beam is the distance from the top (or 
compression flange) to the centroid of area of the tensile steel rein-
forcement. It is indicated by the symbol d.
