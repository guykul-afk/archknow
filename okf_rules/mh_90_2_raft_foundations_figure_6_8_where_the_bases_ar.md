# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 90

• Raft foundations, Figure 6.8, where the bases are combined 
together to form one large base. The raft has to be so reinforced 
to cater for the stresses induced by inequalities of loading and 
bearing capacity.
