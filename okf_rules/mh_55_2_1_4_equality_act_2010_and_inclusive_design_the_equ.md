1.4 Equality Act 2010 and inclusive design 
The Equality Act 2010 (the Act) provides the legal framework in 
Great Britain protecting people from discrimination. It unified a 
range of existing discrimination legislation including supersed-
ing the Disability Discrimination Acts 1995 and 2005 (DDA). It 
is essential in creating and delivering inclusive environments to 
understand the overarching principles of the Act.
