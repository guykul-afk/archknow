# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 76

1.4 Eurocodes 
Eurocode standards will continue to be relevant in the UK until 
the end of 2021 following an agreement between the BSI and the 
European Committee for Standardisation (CEN) to extend the 
arrangement for an extra year beyond the Brexit transition year. 
After then, it is likely that this arrangement will be subject to change.
