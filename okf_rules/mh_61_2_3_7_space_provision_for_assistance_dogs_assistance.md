# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 61

3.7 Space provision for assistance dogs 
Assistance dogs include guide dogs and hearing dogs. While peo-
ple primarily with sensory related disabilities have an assistance 
dog, people with limited mobility and wheelchair users may also 
have an assistance dog. Inclusive Mobility identifies that people 
with assistance dogs require a clear unobstructed width of at least 
1100 mm.
