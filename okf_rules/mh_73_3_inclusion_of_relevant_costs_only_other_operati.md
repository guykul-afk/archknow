# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 73

• Inclusion of relevant costs only – other operational costs that 
would be the same for the two options, cleaning for example, 
can be excluded from the study as long as these assumptions are 
stated and the comparisons normalised.
