# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 86

6.3 Design 
Design of reinforced concrete should be carried out to limit state 
codes in accordance with BS EN 1992–1–1: 2004 + A1: 2014 
Design of concrete structures. General rules and rules for building. 
A succinct guide can be found in How to design concrete structures 
to Eurocode 2 – The Compendium – second edition ( 2018), pre-
pared by MPA The Concrete Centre. Span to depth ratios for slabs 
and beams are indicated in Table XI.
