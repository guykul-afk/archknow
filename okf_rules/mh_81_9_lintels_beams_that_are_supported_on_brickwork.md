# Accessibility Standard: Metric Handbook Accessibility

## Details from Metric Handbook, Page 81

• Lintels. Beams that are supported on brickwork, such as lintels 
over doorways and windows, are not considered structurally 
encastré, and are normally designed as simply supported.
