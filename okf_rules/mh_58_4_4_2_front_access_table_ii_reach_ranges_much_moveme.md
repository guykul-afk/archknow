When considering reach ranges, it is important to consider the 
need for appropriate design solutions that provide for people with 
a range of heights (people of short stature and people above aver-
age height as well as wheelchair users). However, it is important 
to remember that designing features that are easily accessible to a 
wheelchair user may exclude other people, for example, an ambu-
lant disabled person who is unable to bend down easily. More than 
one solution might be required. For instance, if all work surfaces 
are designed for use by wheelchair users, this could have a dispro-
portionately negative ergonomic impact for people of tall stature. 
Therefore, adjustable height work surfaces or a choice of work sur-
face heights should be provided, rather than designing one solution 
for all.
